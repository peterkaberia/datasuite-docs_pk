## `RMNCAH App` Overview

The `RMNCAH App` within the CD2030 Datasuite is designed to support the analysis of routine health facility data for key RMNCAH indicators. 
It provides a standardized framework for transforming raw DHIS2 data into meaningful insights on service coverage, data quality, and equity.

Building on the outputs from the **[Data Extractor](/apps/data-extractor)**, the RMNCAH App guides users through a **[structured analytical workflow](/docs/framework)** that includes data validation, adjustment, and indicator computation. 
This ensures that analyses are consistent, reproducible, and aligned with the **[CD2030 methodological approach](/docs/framework/0-workflow)**.


The app enables users to:
- Assess data quality through standardized DQA metrics  
- Apply numerator and denominator adjustments  
- Generate national and subnational coverage estimates  
- Explore inequalities and trends across time and geography  
- Produce outputs for reporting, including synthesis reports and visualizations  

By integrating methodological rigor with a user-friendly interface, the RMNCAH App facilitates evidence-based decision-making and supports countries in monitoring progress across RMNCAH priorities.
