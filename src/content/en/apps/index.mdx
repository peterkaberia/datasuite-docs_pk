## Datasuite Applications

The CD2030 Datasuite includes a set of integrated applications designed to support the analysis of routine health facility data. 
Each app plays a specific role within the analytical workflow, from data extraction to analysis and reporting.

---


### [CD2030 Data Extractor](/apps/data-extractor)

The Data Extractor connects to DHIS2 systems and allows users to extract and map routine health facility data into standardized formats for analysis.

[Go to Data Extractor →](/apps/data-extractor)

---


### [RMNCAH App](/apps/rmncah)

The RMNCAH App supports analysis across reproductive, maternal, newborn, child, and adolescent health indicators, applying the standardized CD2030 analytical approach.

[Go to RMNCAH App →](/apps/rmncah)
