**`CD2030 DataSuite`** is the dedicated data analytics platform for the **Countdown to 2030** initiative. 
It is designed to empower country and regional health data users (data analysts, researchers, and public health professionals) to track 
progress in women's, children's, and adolescents' health outcomes.
<Bleed>![DataSuite Dashboard](/images/datasuite.jpg)</Bleed>
Through the embedded complimentary applications (**[DHIS2 Data Extractor](/docs/apps/data-extractor), [Vaxx App](/docs/apps/vaxx)** and the **[RMNCAH App](/docs/apps/rmncah)**), the **CD2030 DataSuite** provides users with a one-stop platform for:
- Extraction of RMNCAH indicators from DHIS2, surveys UN, and okther EPI programs datasets 
- Quality assessment and adjustment of the data
- Analysis of the data for various metrics on coverage of MNCH interventions

By seamlessly bridging the gap between routine health facility data (HMIS/DHIS2) and population survey 
estimates, `CD2030 DataSuite` provides a robust, standardized environment for complex statistical analysis that allows users to:
- Confidently assess data quality, 
- Adjust for reporting gaps, 
- Calculate precise/valid coverage denominators, and 
- Generate actionable insights at both national and sub-national levels.
