# Countdown DataSuite

**CD2030 DataSuite** is the dedicated data analytics platform for the **Countdown to 2030** initiative. 
It is designed to empower country and regional health data users (data analysts, researchers, and public health professionals) to track 
progress in women's, children's, and adolescents' health outcomes.
Through the embedded complimentary applications, the **CD2030 DataSuite** provides users with a one-stop platform for:
- Extraction of RMNCAH indicators from DHIS2, surveys UN and other EPI programs datasets 
- Data quality assessment and adjustment of the data
- Analysis of the data for various metrics

By seamlessly bridging the gap between routine health facility data (HMIS/DHIS2) and population survey 
estimates, DataSuite provides a robust, standardized environment for complex statistical analysis. It 
allows users to confidently assess data quality, adjust for reporting gaps, calculate precise coverage
denominators, and generate actionable insights at both national and sub-national levels.
